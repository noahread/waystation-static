About

WELCOME!

The Way Station is a podcast featuring interviews with trailblazers who set the course of their own careers. My name is Noah Read and I host the podcast. The guests have varied interests and come from a wide range of creative and entrepreneurial fields. Join me and learn from those who are cutting a path and following their passions.
